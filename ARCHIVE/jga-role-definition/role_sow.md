# Junior Growth Associate – Bitcoin Ecosystem

**Part-Time (20 hrs/week)**

---

## Overview

We are seeking a motivated and entrepreneurial Junior Growth Associate to support our entry into the Bitcoin mining vertical. This role combines market research, product marketing, channel partnerships, and growth experiments to expand our reach and enable the sales team.

You will work closely with leadership to understand the mining ecosystem, identify new business opportunities, and help create assets and processes that accelerate lead generation. This is a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a fast-moving fintech and Bitcoin infrastructure environment while contributing directly to company growth.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Market Research & Intelligence

- Build a strong understanding of the Bitcoin ecosystem and revenue opportunities: key players, trends, and opportunities. Maintain this in a summary guide/document that we keep up to date. Mining should be the beginning point, but new thesis and tests for growth should be updated (eg: Fede's belief that treasury companies are an opportunity)
- Deliver recommended growth test to be run every week. This should have:
  - Highly defined target list
  - Relevant pains
  - Sample messaging
  - Thesis our expected outcomes
- Synthesize insights into briefs, mapping pain points, personas, and catalysts for outreach along with fast growing segments.

### Product Marketing & Enablement

- Follow up on occasional assets that may need execution with marketing/enablement.
- Draft and refine messaging frameworks with leadership to align product capabilities with miner and partner value propositions.
- Contribute to positioning efforts for miners, hosting providers, infrastructure partners, and related stakeholders.

### Channels & Partnerships

- Explore and evaluate channel partnership opportunities including hosting providers, referral programs, forums, communities, and conference engagements.
- Research and propose partner lists that can drive introductions, referrals, or joint initiatives. Develop recommended plan to achieve leads through building trust and execution.
- Support leadership in testing partnership-driven growth loops and tracking outcomes.

### Growth Experiments

- Maintain and expand target lists of mining and Bitcoin industry decision-makers.
- Run growth experiments (outreach messaging tests, referral loops, community engagement) and document results.
- Test and document AI workflows for research, outreach support, and referral tracking.

### Automation & Reporting

- Provide structured weekly updates on research progress, experiments, and partnership exploration.
- Build and maintain HubSpot reports on outreach engagement and content visibility.
- Recommend automation tools to streamline prospecting and reporting workflows.

---

## Goals (First 90 Days)

1. Deliver proposed test plan for each week accounts per week. This should be a list that a Teampay team member can execute a test outreach campaign to along with a thesis for what is expected.
2. Launch 2–3 growth experiments (outreach tests, referral or partnership pilots).
3. Research and propose channel partnership opportunities with miners, hosting providers, or Bitcoin-related organizations along with the recommended follow through to see referrals come through. (If working correctly, new channels should result in 10 organic channel referrals by 90 days, even if a team member is executing.)
4. Implement a consistent weekly reporting and check-in system for leadership visibility.

---

## Ideal Profile

- Interest in Bitcoin, fintech, and SaaS go-to-market strategies.
- Strong research and writing skills; creative and analytical mindset.
- Comfortable working with AI tools, CRM systems (HubSpot), and automation workflows.
- Organized and independent; able to manage tasks within 20 hours/week.
- 0–2 years of prior experience in growth, marketing, or sales operations preferred.

- Exposure to Bitcoin mining and fintech markets.
- Direct collaboration with company leadership.
- Ownership of research, growth experiments, and partnership development.
- Opportunity to build career-ready deliverables (market maps, enablement assets, messaging frameworks).
- Flexible part-time schedule designed to fit with academic or other commitments.
