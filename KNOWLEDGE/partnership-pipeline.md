# Partnership Pipeline

> **Purpose:** Track partner prospects and active partnerships
> **Update Frequency:** Wednesday (new partners) + Friday (status updates)
> **Used By:** Partner One-Pager (Agent 5), Weekly Exec Update (Agent 6)

---

## Partnership States

| State | Definition | Exit Criteria |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| **Identified** | Partner prospect identified, not yet researched | One-pager completed |
| **Researching** | Researching fit and approach | Ready to reach out |
| **Outreach** | Initial outreach sent | Response received |
| **Conversation** | Active discussion, exploring fit | Partnership terms discussed |
| **Active** | Partnership live, referring/collaborating | Ongoing relationship |
| **Paused** | Not a fit right now, may revisit | Re-engage trigger identified |
| **Closed** | Not a fit, will not pursue | Archived |
